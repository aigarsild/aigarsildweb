Design engineer

I'm Aigar Sild, a design engineer based in Estonia. I design and build digital products — the whole thing, from brand to production code.

Background

I've been doing some version of this for 15 years, starting as a web designer in Tallinn, then working in Denmark and Copenhagen at agencies and product companies, and eventually going independent.

I run two businesses of my own. Kauplus Rattapood is a bicycle shop and workshop that I took from €50–60k to over €100k in annual revenue by rebuilding everything digital. Flowdesk is a workshop management SaaS I designed and built from scratch because no existing tool did what I needed. Both keep me honest — I'm not guessing what works, I'm testing it with my own money.

AI changed how I work. I use it across every project — rapid prototyping, development, automation, research. I've built AI agents that learn from real operational data and drive revenue. I trained with Anthropic's team on getting the most out of Claude. It's the reason I can do what a small team does.

My background is a mix of design and engineering. Bachelor's in Web Development from Denmark, studies in Informatics in Portugal. Four years building e-commerce frontends at Vaimo, product design at Flowit, and independent work since 2020. I've shipped brands, marketing sites, webshops, dashboards, and full SaaS products.

I speak Estonian natively and English fluently.
