Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in a brand identity project?

Logo design (primary mark, secondary versions, icon), color palette, typography selection, visual guidelines, core messaging direction, and an AI Brand Kit your team can use to create on-brand content with ChatGPT or Claude.

Can you also build the website?

Yes — that's actually what makes working with me different. I design the brand and then build the website or product directly from it. One person, one vision. Nothing gets lost between a designer and a developer.

How long does it take?

A brand identity on its own usually takes 1–2 weeks. Brand plus website is typically 2–4 weeks. The exact timeline depends on scope and how quickly you can provide feedback.

What's the AI Brand Kit?

A reference document your team can use with ChatGPT or Claude to write on-brand content — social posts, page copy, emails. It captures your voice, tone, and visual rules so anyone can stay consistent without design help.

How do revisions work?

I include one revision round per phase. I work with one clear direction rather than presenting three competing concepts — it keeps things focused and gets you to a better result faster.

What if I only need a brand, not a website?

That's perfectly fine. Not everyone needs a website right away. I can deliver just the brand identity and AI Brand Kit. If you want a website later, we can pick it up anytime since the brand foundation is already there.
