Kauplus Rattapood

Brand Webshop AI Automation WooCommerce

The Challenge

Kauplus Rattapood, a bicycle shop and workshop in Estonia, needed a complete digital transformation. Their existing online presence wasn't reflecting the quality of their in-store experience, and manual processes were eating into valuable time that could be spent helping customers.

They needed a solution that would not only look professional but also streamline their day-to-day operations through automation.

The Solution

I delivered a comprehensive package covering three core areas:

Brand Identity: A fresh, modern brand that captures the energy of cycling culture while maintaining professionalism. The new identity works across digital and print touchpoints.

E-commerce Platform: A full-featured WooCommerce webshop built for speed and conversion. Product management became effortless with AI-assisted descriptions and categorization.

Visit the webshop →

AI Automation: Custom automation for product data entry, inventory management, and customer communications. What used to take hours now happens automatically.

Flowdesk Integration

Rattapood became one of the first beta users of Flowdesk—a workshop management system I developed. This integration brought real-time booking, job tracking, and customer notifications into one seamless platform.

50-60k€ → 100k€+
Annual revenue growth after implementing the new platform and Flowdesk

The Results

The impact was immediate and substantial. Within the first year:

• Revenue nearly doubled, crossing the €100,000 mark
• Workshop bookings increased through the streamlined online system
• Staff saved 10+ hours weekly on administrative tasks
• Customer satisfaction improved with automated status updates

Ongoing Partnership

Today, I continue working with Rattapood on maintenance, feature updates, and further automation opportunities. The AI-documented codebase means changes are fast and affordable.
