Hykell.com

Brand Website Product Design SaaS

The Challenge

Hykell is a cloud cost optimization platform helping businesses reduce their AWS, Azure, and GCP spending. They needed a complete brand identity and web presence that would communicate technical expertise while remaining approachable to decision-makers who aren't cloud specialists.

The Solution

Brand Identity: Created a modern, tech-forward brand with a distinctive visual language. The color palette balances professional blues with energetic teals, reflecting both trust and innovation.

Marketing Website: Designed and developed a conversion-focused website that clearly explains complex cloud optimization concepts. Interactive elements and data visualizations make the value proposition immediately clear.

Product Design: Crafted the dashboard UI/UX for the main application, focusing on making complex cost data accessible and actionable. Users can quickly identify savings opportunities and track optimization progress.

Key Features

• Clean, data-rich dashboard design
• Interactive cost comparison visualizations
• Streamlined onboarding flow
• Responsive design for all devices
• Dark mode interface for extended use

Ongoing Partnership

I continue working with Hykell on iterative product improvements, new feature designs, and marketing materials. The AI-documented design system ensures consistency across all touchpoints.
