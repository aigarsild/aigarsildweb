Flowdesk.ee

Brand Web App Product Design Next.js

The Challenge

Service workshops—bicycle shops, auto repair, equipment rental—struggle with managing bookings, job tracking, and customer communications. Most solutions are either too complex or don't fit the specific workflows of small service businesses.

The Solution

I designed and developed Flowdesk from the ground up—a workshop management system tailored for service businesses. This is my own product, built based on real needs observed while working with clients like Rattapood.

Brand Identity: Clean, professional branding that communicates efficiency and reliability. The visual language is approachable yet business-focused.

Product Design: Intuitive dashboard interface for managing jobs, bookings, inventory, and customer communications. Every feature was designed with the busy workshop owner in mind.

Technical Stack: Built with Next.js, Supabase, and deployed on Vercel for optimal performance and scalability.

Key Features

• Online booking system with availability management
• Job queue and status tracking
• Customer communication automation
• Inventory management
• Sales and analytics dashboard
• Mobile-responsive for on-the-go access

Visit flowdesk.ee →

Impact

Beta users like Kauplus Rattapood have seen significant operational improvements. The streamlined workflow contributed to their revenue growth from €50-60k to over €100k annually.
